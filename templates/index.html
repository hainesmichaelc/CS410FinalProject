Search IMDB with Natural Language Queries

We have collected a dataset of movies, reviews, casts, crews, etc. Use the search bar below to ask questions and see if we can answer them and provide some useful supplementary links. For example, you could ask any of the following simple questions:

  • When did Titanic premier?
  • What was Leonardo Di Caprio's most hated movie?
  • What was the most popular movie from 2016?

Or, you could ask more complicated questions:

  • What other movies was the actor that played Jack Sparrow in?
  • Who was the actress from Ex Machina and what else has she been in?
  • What movies are the villain from Thor Love and Thunder in?
  • What is the best movie with Rachel from Friends?

We do our best to handle whatever queries you have, but are limited to the data that we have. The more complicated the question, the less likely we will be able to answer.
